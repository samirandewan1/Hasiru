About us

Hasiru

We are a group of nature lovers committed to planting trees, supporting poor farmers, and helping forest guards with essential supplies.

Our Activities

Plantation Drives

Organizing regular plantation events in urban and rural areas.

Support to Farmers

Providing tools, seeds, and basic financial aid to poor farmers.

Forest Guard Aid

Supplying essentials like torches, boots, and jackets to forest guards.

Support to Schools

Providing clean and safe RO drinking water to schools where clean water facilities are not available.

Get Involved

Join our mission! Whether by volunteering or donating, you can make a difference.
